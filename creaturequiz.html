The Fantasy Club Extra Quizzes: Which Creature are You?

In this adventure, you are Taylor, a young adventurer. Some rumors say that Tom Riddle's diary has returned. As part of Dumbledore's Old Army (DOA), you must travel undercover to Hogwarts to find it.The students-including your sister Aquatia-there are in danger. You must destroy the diary before to evacuate before everyone's worst nightmare comes true. You will decide whether He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named can and will return!

"Hi again, Taylor!" Ella, the leader of DOA greets you. "All of those kids are counting on you! Most of all, Harry's counting on you. He'll visit with Ginny once you finish your task. Speaking of your assignment, are you ready for your VERY IMPORTANT MISSION THAT YOU CANNOT FAIL? No pressure!"



Will you take on the perilous duty?
