World Info

A Harry Potter Adventure is an amazing world where dragons and unicorns roam the land of Hogwarts. In this adventure, you are trying to find a way to stop Tom Riddle, who is the deadly Voldemort, from returning. If you fail, Harry Potter's efforts shall all have been in vain. You decide who your friends are. Will it be Brooklyn? Oona? Emma? Kayla? There are countless journies ahead!
